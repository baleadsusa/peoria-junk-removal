Why Hot Tub Removal Is a Job for Professionals

A standard hot tub weighs 500–800 lbs empty — and most aren't in easy-to-access locations. Attempting to move one yourself risks injury, property damage to your deck or yard, and a whole lot of frustration.

Our crew brings the right tools and manpower to disconnect, dismantle, and remove your hot tub safely and efficiently. We haul away all the pieces and leave your space clean.
